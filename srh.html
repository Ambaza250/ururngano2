Ubuzima Bw'Imyororokere

Ese ni iki nakora ngo ngabanye uburibwe mu gihe cy'imihango?

Kubabara mu nda mu gihe cy'imihango ni ibintu bisanzwe ariko iyo ububabare butihanganirwa, ihutire kuya kwa muganga.

Ibintu byagufasha:

  • Gukoresha amazi ashyushye ku nda cyangwa igitambaro cy'ubushyuhe
  • Imyitozo ngororamubiri yoroheje nk'ugendagenda cyangwa yoga
  • Kurya indyo yuzuye no kunywa amazi menshi
  • Kuruhuka no guhumeka byimbitse

Ibimenyetso by'imihango

Ibimenyetso bisanzwe: kubabara umutwe, kubabara mu nda, kubyimba amabere, umunaniro, n'impinduka mu myifatire.

Kuri bwa munda mu gihe cy'imihango ni ibintu bisanzwe ritabwo ari uburwayi ariko igihe ububare butihanganirwa kugeraho bituma udakora inshingano zawe za buri munsi, ihutire kuya kwa muganga bagufashe banakugabanyirize ububabare.

  • Gukoresha amazi ashyushye mu kwivura
  • Koga amazi ashyushye
  • Imyitozo ngororamubiri yoroheje
  • Kwirinda umwuma no kurya indyo yuzuye

Umukobwa cyangwa umugore aba afite amahirwe menshi yo gusama iyo ari mu gihe cy'uburumbuke.

Uburumbuke ni igihe igi rirekurwa mu mirantanga.

Igi rirekurwa k'umunsi wa 14 duturutse inyuma (ku munsi wa nyuma w'ukwezi k'umugore).

Virusi itera sida (HIV)

VIH ni virusi yibasira ubudahangarwa bw'umubiri. Iyo itavuwe, ishobora gutera SIDA.

Uko yanduramo:

  • Imibonano mpuzabitsina idakingiye
  • Gusangira ibikoresho byanduye
  • Kuva ku nyina ku mwana

Uko wirinda:

  • Gukoresha agakingirizo buri gihe
  • Kwipimisha VIH
  • Kugira umukunzi umwe gusa
