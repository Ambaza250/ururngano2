Ubuzima Bw' Imyororokere

Menya, gusobanukirwa no kwita ku buzima bwawe bw'imyororokere

Imihango ni ikimenyetso gisanzwe kigaragaza ko umubiri w'umukobwa uri gukura neza kandi uva mu bwana ujya mu bukuru. Ibaho iyo nyababyeyi iba yakoze imyiteguro yo kwakira inda ariko ntihaboneke gusama, bityo icyo cyari cyateguwe kigasenuka kikava mu mubiri mu ishusho y'amaraso anyura mu gitsina. Ubusanzwe imihango imara hagati y'iminsi 3 na 5, nubwo hari abakobwa cyangwa abagore bayigira igihe gito cyangwa kirekire kurushaho. Iyo imihango irengeje iminsi 7, ni byiza kwihutira kugana umukozi w'ubuzima.
Ibintu by'ingenzi: • Imihango si uburwayi • Hari abakobwa bayitangira ku myaka 9 cyangwa 10, abandi bakayibona nyuma nko ku myaka 16 • Gukurikira ukwezi kwawe bigufasha kwitegura no gusobanukirwa umubiri wawe • Umukobwa watangiye kujya mu mihango ashobora gusama akoze imibonano mpuzabitsina idakingiye
Abakobwa n'abagore benshi bagira impinduka z'umubiri n'iz'amarangamutima mbere y'uko imihango itangira. Ibi bimenyetso bishobora gutangira icyumweru kimwe cyangwa bibiri mbere y'imihango, bikanakomeza mu minsi mike nyuma y'uko itangiye. Umuntu ashobora kugira umutwe, kubabara mu kiziba cy'inda, kubyimba cyangwa kuribwa amabere, umunaniro, gutura umubi, ibiheri, impinduka mu mirire, agahinda, kurakara, kubura ibitotsi cyangwa impinduka mu byiyumviro bijyanye n'imibonano mpuzabitsina. Ibi bimenyetso ni ibisanzwe kandi akenshi ntibisobanura uburwayi keretse bibaye bikabije bikabangamira ubuzima bwa buri munsi.
Ibintu by'ingenzi: • Ibimenyetso biratandukana bitewe n'umuntu • Imyitozo yoroheje, indyo yuzuye, kuruhuka no kugabanya stress birafasha • Niba ibi bimenyetso bikabije cyangwa bikabangamira ubuzima bwawe, jya kwa muganga
Kubabara mu gihe cy'imihango bishobora kuba ibisanzwe, ariko iyo ububabare bukabije kugeza aho butuma udakora inshingano zawe za buri munsi, ni ngombwa gushaka ubufasha kwa muganga. Uburyo bworoshye bwo kugabanya ububabare harimo gushyira ubushyuhe butekanye ku kiziba cy'inda, koga amazi ashyushye, gukora imyitozo yoroheje nko kugenda cyangwa kwiyorora, kunywa amazi ahagije, kurya indyo yuzuye, kuruhuka bihagije no gukora ibikorwa bituma utuza nko guhumeka cyane cyangwa meditation. Hari n'abafashwa no gukanda buhoro ahagana mu mugongo wo hasi cyangwa ku kiziba cy'inda.
Ibintu by'ingenzi: • Ikawa nyinshi, isukari nyinshi cyangwa ibinyobwa birimo umunyu bishobora kongera uburibwe ku bantu bamwe • Nta buryo bumwe bukorera buri wese • Niba ububabare bukabije, ntuceceke; saba ubufasha hakiri kare
Umukobwa cyangwa umugore aba afite amahirwe menshi yo gusama mu gihe cy'uburumbuke. Icyo ni igihe intangangore iba irekuwe iva mu ibere ry'intanga kandi ishobora guhura n'intangangabo. Mu kwezi kwa benshi, kurekurwa kw'igi kuba hafi y'iminsi 14 mbere y'imihango ikurikira, nubwo ukwezi kudasa ku bantu bose. Ku bafite ukwezi kudahindagurika, igihe cy'uburumbuke gikunze kubarwa nk'iminsi igera kuri 7. Nta munsi ushobora gufatwa nk'umutekano wuzuye ku kutazisama igihe habaye imibonano mpuzabitsina idakingiye, kuko imibiri n'ukwezi bitandukana.
Ibintu by'ingenzi: • Gusama bisaba uruhare rw'intangangore n'intangangabo • Igihe cyo kurekura igi gishobora guhinduka cyane cyane ku bafite ukwezi guhindagurika • Gukurikira imihango yawe bigufasha gusobanukirwa neza iminsi y'uburumbuke
VIH ni virusi yibasira uturemangingo turinda umubiri cyane cyane utwa CD4, igatuma ubudahangarwa bw'umubiri bugabanuka. Iyo itavuwe ishobora gutera SIDA. VIH ishobora kwandurira mu mibonano mpuzabitsina idakingiye, gusangira ibikoresho bikomeretsa byanduye amaraso, kwakira amaraso yanduye, cyangwa ikava ku mubyeyi ikajya ku mwana igihe atwite, abyara cyangwa yonsa. VIH ntandurira mu gusuhuzanya, gusangira amafunguro cyangwa mu busabane busanzwe.
Ibintu by'ingenzi: • Iyo itavuwe, VIH ishobora guca intege umubiri cyane • Ingaruka zayo zishobora kuba indwara zihoraho, umunaniro, kugabanuka ibiro, umuriro no kugira ibibazo by'uruhu • Amakuru y'ukuri afasha urubyiruko kwirinda
Urubyiruko rwakwihagararaho mu kwirinda VIH ruhitamo imyitwarire irinda ubuzima nko kwifata, ubudahemuka, kwirinda imibonano mpuzabitsina idakingiye, gukoresha agakingirizo neza, kutagabana ibikoresho bikomeretsa no kwipimisha kenshi kugira ngo rumenye uko ruhagaze. Ni ingenzi kandi kwirinda igitutu cy'urungano, kwibanda ku masomo n'intego nziza, gushaka amakuru yizewe no kwanga ivangura rikorerwa ababana na VIH. Kwirinda VIH bitangirira ku byemezo bifite amakuru no kubaha ubuzima bwawe n'ubw'abandi.
Ibintu by'ingenzi: • Kwipimisha VIH kenshi bigufasha kumenya uko ubuzima bwawe buhagaze hakiri kare • Ntukemere ko igitutu cy'urungano kigushyira mu byemezo bishobora kukugirira nabi • Urubyiruko rukwiye kugana abizewe n'inzobere igihe rukeneye amakuru nyayo ku buzima bw'imyororokere
