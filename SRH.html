Ibikorwa by'Ubuzima bw'Imyororokere (SRH Services)

Amakuru yizewe kandi yoroshye kumenya

Ese ni iki nakora ngo ngabanye uburibwe mu gihe cy'imihango?

Kubabara mu nda mu gihe cy’imihango ni ibintu bisanzwe, ariko iyo ububabare butihanganirwa, ihutire kuya kwa muganga bagufashe.

Ibintu byagufasha guhangana n’ububabare:

  • Gukoresha amazi ashyushye: Gushyira ubushyuhe ku nda cyangwa koga amazi ashyushye bishobora koroshya imikaya no kugabanya ububabare. Koresha icupa ririmo amazi ashyushye cyangwa igitambaro cy’amazi ishyushye mu gihe cy’iminota 15-20.
  • Imyitozo ngororamubiri yoroheje: Kugendagenda, kwigorora (Yoga) cyangwa imyitozo yoroheje bifasha amaraso gutembera neza.
  • Kurya indyo yuzuye: Kurya ibiryo byiza, guhagarika ikawa nyinshi, inzoga n’ibinyobwa birimo umunyu mwinshi.
  • Kunywa amazi menshi: Byibura litro ebyiri ku munsi kugira ngo wirinde umwuma.
  • Kuruhuka no guhumeka byimbitse: Meditation cyangwa deep breathing bigabanya stress.
  • Gukanda ahantu habugenewe: Gukanda buhoro buhoro ku nda cyangwa mu mugongo.

Ese ni ibihe bimenyetso bishobora kunyereka ko ngiye kujya mu mihango?

Ibimenyetso bibanziriza imihango bitangira mu cyumweru kimwe cyangwa bibiri mbere.

  • Kubabara umutwe, kubabara mu kiziba cy’inda
  • Kubabara no kubyimba amabere
  • Gutura umubi, kugira umunaniro
  • Kumva udashaka kurya cyangwa ushaka kurya cyane
  • Kumva ubabaye cyangwa udatuje

Ni ryari umukobwa cyangwa umugore aba afite amahirwe menshi yo gusama?

Umukobwa cyangwa umugore aba afite amahirwe menshi yo gusama mu gihe cy’**uburumbuke** (ovulation).

Uburumbuke ni igihe intangangore (igi) irekurwa mu mirantanga. Igihe cy’uburumbuke gitera amahirwe yo gusama ari ku munsi wa 14 w’ukwezi (ku bakobwa bafite ukwezi kudahindagurika).

Igihe cy’uburumbuke gishobora kumara iminsi 5 mbere y’uburumbuke n’umunsi umwe nyuma yabwo.

Virusi itera sida (HIV / SIDA)

VIH ni virusi yibasira ubudahangarwa bw’umubiri. Iyo itavuwe, ishobora gutera indwara yitwa SIDA.

Uko yanduramo:

  • Gukora imibonano mpuzabitsina idakingiye
  • Gusangira ibikoresho by’amaraso byanduye
  • Kwanduzwa n’umubyeyi ku mwana

Uko wirinda VIH:

  • Gukoresha agakingirizo buri gihe
  • Kugira umukunzi umwe gusa
  • Kwipimisha VIH
  • Gukoresha PrEP na PEP

Ubutumwa bw'ingenzi:

Kwipimisha ni byiza kandi ni ngombwa. Niba ufite ubwoba cyangwa ibibazo, hamagara 3530 cyangwa uje kwa muganga hakiri kare.
