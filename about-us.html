About Urungano Platform

Urungano ni urubuga rw'ubufasha rw'ubuzima bw'imyororokere n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe, rwubakiye ku banyarwanda kandi rukoresha Kinyarwanda nk'ururimi rw'ibanze.

Ubutumwa bwacu

Urungano Platform yashinzwe kugira ngo iboneze ubufasha bw'ubuzima bw'imyororokere (SRH) n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe mu buryo bworoshye, bwizewe, kandi butagira akato. Twifuza ko buri mukobwa, umugore n'umuryango wose yabona amakuru n'ubufasha atagomba kwihanganira ubwoba cyangwa isoni.

Vision yacu

Kuba urubuga rw'ibanze rw'ubufasha mu Rwanda rw'ubuzima bw'imyororokere n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe, aho buri muntu yabona ubufasha bwizewe, bw'umwuga, kandi mu rurimi yumva neza (Kinyarwanda n'icyongereza).

Mission yacu

Gutanga amakuru yizewe, ubufasha bwa AI, n'uburyo bwo gukurikirana ubuzima bw'imyororokere mu buryo bworoshye kandi bwa Kinyarwanda, kugira ngo dukumire indwara, duteze imbere uburinganire, kandi dufashe abantu kwiyubaha no kwirinda.

Urifuza kuba hamwe natwe?
