Ibitwerekeye Urungano Platform

Urubuga rw'ubufasha rwizewe, rw'umwihariko mu Kinyarwanda, rutagira akato ku bijyanye n'ubuzima bw'imyororokere n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe.

Amakuru kuri Twe (About Us)

Urungano ni urubuga rw'ikoranabuhanga rwashyiriweho korohereza urubyiruko rw'u Rwanda kubona amakuru k'ubuzima bw'imyororokere (SRH) hamwe n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe muburyo hifashishijwe Telefone ngendanwa na mudasobwa. Twibanda cyane ku gukuraho inzitizi z’isoni n’itotezwa, tugamije ko buri muntu wese, hibandwa kubafite ubumuga, abona amakuru yizewe mu muhezo no mu bwisanzure binyuze kuri internet(Urungano AI, content creation) cyangwa ubutumwa bugufi (SMS/USSD).

Inshingano (Mission)

Inshingano yacu ni ukubaka umuryango w’urubyiruko rufite amakuru ahagije ku buzima bw'imyororokere n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe hibandwa kubafite ubumuga, binyuze mu gukoresha ikoranabuhanga ry'ubwenge buhangano (Artificial Intelligence) n’uburyo bw’itumanaho bugera kuri bose (Ubutumwa bugufi,USSD), kugira ngo dutange ubufasha bwihuse, bwizewe kandi butandukanya amateka n’ukuri kuri bose.

Intego (Objectives)

  • Gutanga amakuru n’ubujyanama ku buzima bwo mu mutwe no kwirinda indwara zandurira mu mibonano mpuzabitsina (STIs/HIV).
  • Gufasha abakobwa n'abagore uburyo bwo gukurikirana ukwezi k'umugore (Period tracking) n’uburumbuke mu buryo bworoshye.
  • Guharanira ko abafite ubumuga (abatabona, abatumva) babona amakuru mu buryo bwihariye (Hifashishijwe amarenga, Indangururamajwi byubakanywe nuru rubuga).
  • Gufasha urubyiruko guhangana n'ibibazo byo mu mutwe nk'agahinda gakabije, kwigunga, kwiheba binyuze hifashishijwe ubwenge buhangano AI ndetse na ganira n'urungano (Uruganiriro rwibanga ruhuza abakoresha uru rubuga, bakaganira kubibazo bahira nabyo, bagahumurizanya ndetse bagafashanya kubivamo).
  • Kwizeza abakoresha urubuga rwacu ko amakuru yabo n'ibyo babaza ubwenge buhangano (AI) bigirwa ibanga rikomeye.
