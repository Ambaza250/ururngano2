Ibitwerekeye Urungano Platform

Urubuga rw'ubufasha rwizewe, rw'umwihariko mu Kinyarwanda, rutagira akato ku bijyanye n'ubuzima bw'imyororokere n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe.

Ubutumwa bwacu

Urungano Platform yashinzwe kugira ngo iboneze ubufasha bw'ubuzima bw'imyororokere n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe mu buryo bworoshye, bwizewe, kandi butagira akato. Twifuza ko buri mukobwa, umugore n'umuryango wose yabona amakuru n'ubufasha atagomba kwihanganira ubwoba cyangwa isoni.

Vision yacu

Kuba urubuga rw'ibanze rw'ubufasha mu Rwanda rw'ubuzima bw'imyororokere n'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe, aho buri muntu yabona ubufasha bwizewe, bw'umwuga, kandi mu rurimi yumva neza.

Mission yacu

Gutanga amakuru yizewe, ubufasha bwa AI, n'uburyo bwo gukurikirana ukwezi mu buryo bworoshye mu Kinyarwanda n'icyongereza, kugira ngo dukumire indwara, duteze imbere uburinganire, kandi dufashe abantu kwiyubaha no kwirinda.
