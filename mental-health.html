Ubuzima bwo mu Mutwe

Amakuru yizewe kugira ngo ufashe umutwe wawe n’uw’abandi

Isomo rya 1: Gusobanukirwa Ubuzima bwo mu Mutwe

Ubuzima bwo mu mutwe ni uburyo utekereza, uko wiyumva, n’uko ubana n’abandi. Si "ubusazi", ni imigendekere myiza y’intekerezo zawe.

Ibimenyetso by’ibanze: Kutaryama neza, kumva uhorana umunzi (stress), kwiheba, no kwigunga cyane.

Inama: Niba wiyumva nabi mu mutwe, si intege nke. Vuga ibikubaho! Kuganira n’inshuti cyangwa inzobere ni intambwe ya mbere yo gukira.

Isomo rya 2: Guhangana n’Imihangayiko n’Agahinda

Uburyo bwo kuruhura umutwe: Hagarara gato, uhumeke utura (deep breathing), utekereze ku bintu bito bigushimisha.

Kwirinda inzira mbi: Inzoga n’ibiyobyabwenge ntibikemura ibibazo, ahubwo birabyongera. Shaka uburyo bwiza bwo kwishimira ubuzima (siporo, muzika, gusoma).

Icyizere: Wibuke ko buri muntu wese agira ibihe bikomeye. Kwemera ko ukeneye ubufasha ni ubutwari, si ikimwaro.

Isomo rya 3: Ubuzima bwo mu Mutwe n’Imyororokere

Ihungabana n’inda zitateganyijwe: Umwangavu wasamye inda akenera gushyigikirwa mu mutwe kurusha kunenwa. Agahinda n’ubwoba bishobora kuvurwa binyuze mu mubujyanama (Counseling).

Gufata ibyemezo bituje: Igihe ufite ibishuko mu mibonano mpuzabitsina, banza utekereze ku ngaruka zabyo ku mutima n'ahazaza hawe.

Isomo rya 4: Uburyo bwo Kubona Ubufasha n’Inshingano

Niba ufite ibitekerezo byo kwigirira nabi, agahinda kamaze ibyumweru birenga bibiri, cyangwa kumva utagishaka kubaho, shaka ubufasha uyu munsi.

Aho wakura ubufasha:

  • Hamagara 114 (RBC) cyangwa 112 mu bihe byihutirwa
  • Gana ikigo cy’ubuzima cyangwa ibitaro bikuri hafi
  • Vugana n’umujyanama mu by’ubuzima bwo mu mutwe
