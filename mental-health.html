Ubuzima bwo mu Mutwe

Menya, gusobanukirwa no kwita ku buzima bwawe bwo mu mutwe

Umuntu ashobora kugira agahinda, ubwoba, kwigunga cyangwa guhangayika nk'igice cy'ubuzima busanzwe, ariko iyo ibyo bimara igihe kirekire bikatangira kubangamira imibereho n'imikorere ye ya buri munsi, bishobora kuba ibimenyetso by'uburwayi bwo mu mutwe. Uburwayi bwo mu mutwe ni imyitwarire, amarangamutima cyangwa imitekerereze idasanzwe bituma umuntu atabasha guhangana n'ibibazo bisanzwe by'ubuzima cyangwa kubikemura neza.

Bimwe mu bibazo bikunze kugaragara mu buzima bwo mu mutwe harimo indwara y'agahinda gakabije, ihungabana, indwara z'umuhangayiko, schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, ibibazo byo mu mikurire, igicuri, ibibazo bifitanye isano n'ibiyobyabwenge n'izindi ndwara zihindura uko umuntu atekereza, yiyumva cyangwa yitwara.

Uburwayi bwo mu mutwe bushobora guterwa n'impamvu nyinshi zitandukanye. Harimo imiterere y'umuryango cyangwa uruhurirane rw'indwara, ubukene, intambara, ihohoterwa, kwimurwa, kubura ab'agaciro, uburwayi bw'umubiri budakira, amakimbirane yo mu muryango, guhohoterwa n'ikoreshwa ry'ibiyobyabwenge. Hari kandi ibikomere byo mu bwana nko gutabwa, kutitabwaho, gufatwa ku ngufu, gutandukana kw'ababyeyi, gutwara inda ukiri muto ugatereranwa n'ibindi bikomeretsa amarangamutima bishobora kongera ibyago.

Uburwayi bwo mu mutwe bushobora kugaragarira ku mubiri, mu marangamutima, mu bitekerezo cyangwa mu myitwarire. Ibimenyetso byo ku mubiri bishobora kuba uburibwe, umunaniro uhoraho nubwo umuntu yaba yararuhutse, kurwara umutwe kenshi, kubura ibitotsi cyangwa gusinzira cyane. Mu marangamutima hashobora kubamo agahinda gakabije, guhangayika, ubwoba cyangwa kwiheba. Mu myitwarire hashobora kugaragara uburakari, kwiheza, kwigunga cyangwa kugerageza kwiyangiza. Hari n'abashobora kumva amajwi, kubona ibyo abandi batabona, kumva bakurikiranywe cyangwa kunanirwa gutekereza neza no gufata imyanzuro.

Kumenya ibi bimenyetso hakiri kare bifasha umuntu kwihutira gushaka ubufasha cyangwa gufasha undi mbere y'uko ikibazo kirushaho gukomera.

Ntibyoroshye buri gihe kumenya ko umuntu atekereza kwiyahura kuko akenshi ahisha ibiri muri we. Ariko hari ibimenyetso bikunze kugaragara: kumva ababaye cyane, kudatuza, kwigunga, kubona ubuzima nta gaciro bufite, kuvuga cyangwa kwandika ku rupfu, kuba yarigeze kugerageza kwiyahura, kunywa ibisindisha cyane, gukora ibintu bishyira ubuzima mu kaga, kuvuga ko azagenda bakamubura cyangwa kumva ari umutwaro ku bandi.

Niba wibonyeho ibi bimenyetso cyangwa ukabibona ku nshuti yawe, ntubyirengagize. Vugana n'umuntu wizewe ako kanya kandi mushake ubufasha ku ivuriro rikwegereye cyangwa ku muntu wabihuguriwe.

Iyo ufashe umuntu ugaragaza ibimenyetso by'uburwayi bwo mu mutwe, tangira umutege amatwi neza. Muganirire ahantu hatuje kandi hihereye, umukirane urugwiro, umuhe umwanya uhagije kandi ugire ibanga ku byo akubwiye. Gutega amatwi neza bifasha umuntu kumva atekanye kandi yumviswe.

Mu gihe umuteze amatwi, wirinde kwihutira gutanga inama n'ibisubizo, wirinde kumutera ubwoba cyangwa kumucira urubanza, ntumuhatire kuvuga ibyo adashaka kuvuga, kandi ntukarangare ukoresha telefoni cyangwa ngo usohoke hagati mu kiganiro. Ubufasha bw'ihuse no kumuhuza n'ubuvuzi bukwiye bishobora gutuma akira cyangwa akoroherwa agakomereza ubuzima bwe mu gaciro.

Niba wowe cyangwa undi muntu mukeneye ubufasha bwihuse, serivisi z'ubuzima bwo mu mutwe ziboneka ku bigo nderabuzima, mu bitaro no kuri nimero 114.

Isuzuma ry'Ubuzima bwo mu Mutwe

Hitamo uburyo bwo kwisuzuma ibimenyetso umaze iminsi wumva.

Iri suzuma rigamije kugufasha kwisuzuma gusa. Ntirisimbura muganga, umuhanga mu by'imitekerereze cyangwa umujyanama. Niba wumva uri mu kaga cyangwa ufite ibitekerezo byo kwiyangiza, ihutire gushaka ubufasha.
